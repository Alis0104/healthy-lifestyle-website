Loaded Avocado toast

by DENAKLOSTERMAN.

WHAT IS SO SPECIAL ABOUT LOADED AVOCADO TOAST?

Never has avocado toast tasted so good! Beautiful and healthy, the best of both worlds! This Loaded Avocado Toast is so fresh and savory. The flavorful ingredients of Cavender’s All Purpose Greek Seasoning, Balsamic Glaze, Frigo Crumbled Feta Cheese, Avocado, Cherry Tomatoes, Fresh Cilantro, and a toasted French Baguette is so delectable.

HOW TO MAKE LOADED AVOCADO TOAST

STEP ONE

1. Begin by microwaving peas.

2.Next, toast the slices of baguette in toaster.

3.Then slice cherry tomatoes and avocado lengthwise.

4.Place slices of avocado and cherry tomatoes beautifully on toast.

5.Rinse and chop cilantro and place on toast.

6.Add Feta cheese and peas as well.

7. with balsamic glaze.

8.Eat and enjoy!

INGREDIENTS IN LOADED AVOCADO TOAST

4 Cherry Tomatoes

1 Tbsp. Crumbled Feta Cheese

1 Avocado

10 Leaves of Cilantro

1 Tbsp. Cavender’s All Purpose Greek Seasoning

2 Tbsp. Peas (I had leftover microwave peas)

3 Slices French Baguette

Drizzle of Balsamic Glaze
